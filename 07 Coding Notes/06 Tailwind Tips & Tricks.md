## Tailwind Tips & Tricks

- Change accent color:
	```html
	<input type="checkbox" class="accent-pink-500" checked />
	```
- Fluid texts
	```html
	<p class="text-[min(10vw,60px)]">Tea and Botanical Solutions</p>
	```
